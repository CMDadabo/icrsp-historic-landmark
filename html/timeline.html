A Timeline of Progress

Today, the church is no longer the empty shell that it once was, but the project of rehabilitation is still only in its very beginnings, and progress is entirely dependent on the generous donation of the needed funds. Since this historic landmark was adopted by the Institute of Christ the King, we have made great progress in restoring it to its former glory.

New Organ Installed

October 2013

A new Hauptwerk organ was installed to continue the master plan of renovating the entire organ and pipes. The new organ produces beautiful music through a fusion of mechanical and digital technology.

Vestibule Renovation

September 2013

Ground was broken in the vestibule renovation. The first step in the create of a new first floor bathroom (pictured) to make the Shrine ADA compliant.

Insulation Installation

January 2013

The original insulation, stripped away long ago, was replaced, to ensure that the Shrine will remain a cultural and spiritual hub in all seasons.
