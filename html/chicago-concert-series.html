Experience the Beauty of Culture

The Shrine of Christ the King, a landmark church which had an empty, gutted interior two years ago, is already today a cultural center where art, music, and architecture offer a glimpse of divine beauty. Located just minutes from Chicago's Lakefront and the Museum of Science and Industry, this extraordinary edifice is now a venue for a remarkable program of classical concerts and lectures on history and the arts. Inspiring the mind and uplifting the heart, these enriching experiences of cultural beauty are attracting a growing number of people from the local Chicago community and beyond. "Hidden Treasures" Concert Series

The Shrine offers a program of baroque chamber concerts featuring the lesser known masterpieces of great composers. In these one-hour concerts without intermission, you can discover a hidden treasure — delightful music which is rarely performed elsewhere! Hear a sampling from the videos below.
